About me

I am a first year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Maryland, College Park. I am advised by Dr. Rama Chellappa. My research interests are in the areas of Computer Vision and Machine Learning.

Previously, I obtained a Bachelor's degree (B.Tech), with a major in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Operations Research from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras.
